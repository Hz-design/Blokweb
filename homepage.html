Welcome to Seoul Sista

Seoul Sista is the new talk of the town in Rotterdam. At Korean barbecue you prepare your own meat and vegetables on a grill that is built into the table. We are an all-you-can-eat restaurant where you can dine for a fixed price. This allows you to enjoy all our dishes and discover the Korean cuisine.

We are open from 12pm to 5pm (closed on Tuesdays) and operate 2 shifts. In the overview below you can see our prices.

If you come by car, you can easily park at our neighbors of the Apcoa Parking Westblaak.

The Korean Kitchen

한국 음식

Korean cuisine has a number of important aspects. Marinated meat, such as Bulgogi Beef, which literally means “Fire Meat”. Grill the Bulgogi Beef at the table and top with Korean Doenjang, a soybean paste, and wrap in a fresh leaf of lettuce.

Banchan is the name of the side dishes that are served with a Korean meal. Kimchi, spicey fermented white cabbage is the best known.

In addition, we offer many other variations such as Bibimbap, rice bowls, or Stews such as our short Rib or Seafood soups.
